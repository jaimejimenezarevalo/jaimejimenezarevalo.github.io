About Me

I am a mathematics student interested in algebraic coding theory, finite fields, linear algebra, algebraic geometry, and computational mathematics.

My current work explores affine Grassmann codes, symmetric matrices over finite fields, and computational experiments using SageMath and Python.

Research Interests

  • Finite fields

Selected Projects

Symmetric Matrices over F₃

A computational investigation of symmetric matrices over the finite field F₃, including rank classification, partitions of the field, and enumeration using SageMath.

Affine Grassmann Codes

An ongoing student research project studying the structure and decoding of affine Grassmann codes through algebraic and computational methods.

Finite-Field Matrix Enumeration

SageMath programs that generate and classify matrices over finite fields according to symmetry, rank, and prescribed field partitions.
